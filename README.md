## Methodology

Every writeup and lab document in this portfolio follows a consistent structure:

- **Enumeration first** — no assumptions, every port and service documented before exploitation begins
- **Tool breakdown** — every flag explained so the reasoning behind each command is clear
- **Real output** — actual terminal output from the lab, not sanitised or fabricated
- **Kill chain context** — each step explained in terms of where it sits in the overall attack path
- **Lessons learned** — what surprised me, what failed first, and what I would do differently in a real engagement

The standard applied here is the same standard I intend to bring to professional penetration testing work.
